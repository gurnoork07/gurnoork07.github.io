Hi! My name is Gurnoor Kaur, and I'm a robotics student with a CS minor at the University of Michigan. This site showcases my exciting journey through the EECS 440 Extended Reality projects.

Project 1: Late Night Lab Adventure

Welcome to my first project, crafted using Unreal Engine and the Meta Quest Pro. I worked with two other students to create an immersive simulation that captures the experience of being a student working late nights BBB computer lab.

As you explore the lab, you’ll encounter a cast of characters that bring the environment to life, and if you’re adventurous enough, you might just discover a secret lounge room hidden within! Ready to dive in? Look here to find it!
